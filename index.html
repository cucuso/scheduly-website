The state of the art calendar that notifies your customers with text.

A scheduler for today's world.

Life now a days is hectic. We spend all day on the go. And most of the time we do not have time to answer a call. We might be in the middle of a meeting, or order food etc. Todays world demands a new way of reminding customers when their meeting is coming up. And what is more unobtrusive than a text. You know it wont be lost like an email, or a call, but they will open it on their own terms.

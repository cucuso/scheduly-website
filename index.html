A scheduler for today's world


Nowadays, life is hectic. We spend all day on the go. And most of the time we don't even have time to answer a call. We might be in the middle of a meeting, ordering food, etc. Today's world demands a new way of reminding customers when their meeting is coming up. Surprise your customers with a friendly reminder via text message. And free up your time to do more meaningful work than calling every appointment to confirm.

Click Try Now and sign up to get 15 days for free. You will be able to add appointments and those customers will be notified the day before their appointment via text message.

How it works


Select Appointment
To select any appointment or meeting double click on the date in the calendar.

Enter Information
Enter the phone number, description of the appointment, and time.

Edit Information
If you wish to change any details you can double click again to edit, or press delete on keyboard to delete all together.

Thats it!
Sit back and let us do the rest. The day before the appointment Scheduly will send your customer a text message reminding them they have an appointment the next day.
